For home-game and study use only. Most casinos, cardrooms, and online poker sites prohibit real-time assistance tools during supervised/regulated play. This tool is meant for casual home games and learning how equity and pot odds work — not for use at a supervised table.

How This Works

  1. Enter your two hole cards.
  2. Pick the board stage and enter the community cards you can see (none before the flop).
  3. Optionally enter how many opponents are in the hand, and the pot size / bet you're facing.
  4. Click "Calculate Odds" — the tool simulates thousands of random outcomes to estimate your win probability, then compares it to the equity required by the pot odds (if you entered them) and explains the recommendation in plain English.

v1 limitation: opponents are assumed to hold random hands (no range modeling yet) — this is a simplification that keeps the tool fast and simple, but real opponents don't play every hand randomly.
